Acknowledgements

We express our heart-felt gratitude to the Indian Council of Historical Research (ICHR), for having awarded the glorious project to us.

We are very thankful to the then Chairman of ICHR, Prof. Y.Sudershan Rao, for initially supporting this project having appreciated its intrinsic value. We also thank Prof. Arvind P. Jamkhedkar, current Chairman of the ICHR, who is an eminent Archaeologist and Historian too, for having supported this project on behalf of ICHR.

We are specially beholden to Prof. Kumar Ratnam, honourable Member-Secretary of the ICHR for granting us extra time than what was originally stipulated and encouraging us to complete the project. He has been a pillar of support and also a constant source of inspiration in helping reach the current project to its logical conclusion. He is not only an eminent historian, but also a great scholar in various branches of the Indian Knowledge systems. We convey our heartfelt thanks to him for all his support in the past and his anticipated future support in reaching the results of this Project to the masses, especially those who are interested in various aspects of authentic, undistorted Indian history.

We also place on record our sincere thanks to Dr. Rajesh Kumar, Deputy Director and Mr. Nitin Kumar, Assistant Director and the other officer-bearers of the ICHR for their support and guidance during various stages of the Project.

We also thank all the persons associated with the Samskriti FoundationÂ®, Mysore involved in the execution of the current Project and help it attain its current form. Finally, we thank one and all who have directly or indirectly helped in the successful implementation of this project.

We dedicate the results of this Project to all those discerning persons who propose to study any aspect of Indian History in an authentic and uninhibited manner, since the results of this Project facilitate such a study, by giving in one place, access to hundreds of authentic documents on various aspects of Indian History.
