Astronomy, the study of celestial objects such as stars, planets, galaxies, and their influence on our planet. The earliest references to astronomy appear in the Rig Veda dating back to Vedic times. Sages and scholars like Yagnavalkya, Aryabhatta, Varahamihira, and Bhaskaracharya made significant contributions to this field. The distance between the Sun and Earth and the time it taken for sunlight to reach our planet were calculated accurately by our scholars. These insights highlight the profound contributions of our ancestors to the fascinating field of astronomy.
